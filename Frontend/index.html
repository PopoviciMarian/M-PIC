About

Welcome to M-PIC!
This site was made with the intent to help you gather up photos and information from various social medias, such as pictures, likes, shares. After connecting with the aforementioned social networks, you can edit your photos using filters and making collages. Afterwards you can choose to save them publicly or privately on your M-PIC profile. Enjoy!
